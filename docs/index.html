Auto Unmute for Google Meet

Forgot you were muted? Auto Unmute detects when you start talking and turns your mic back on automatically.

Source on GitHub Privacy Policy

How it works

  • Watches your camera for mouth movement (via face-api.js) and your microphone for recognised speech (via the Web Speech API).
  • When you have been talking for ~400 ms while muted, it dispatches the same Ctrl+D / ⌘+D shortcut you would press yourself.
  • Never re-mutes you on its own. Only you decide when to mute again.
  • 100% local. No data leaves your browser.
